## How to Use
- **Tea Bags**: Simply steep the tea bag in hot water for 3-5 minutes (adjust steeping time to your taste).
- **Loose Tea**: Add approximately 1 teaspoon of loose tea per 8 oz of water, steep for 3-5 minutes, and adjust based on desired strength.
- **Iced Tea**: Brew tea bags or use ready-to-drink iced tea for an instant refreshment.
- **Herbal Tea**: Steep the herbal blend for 4-7 minutes for a soothing, caffeine-free experience.

## How to Use the Store Locator

You can quickly locate stores near you that stock Lipton tea by using the **Store Locator** feature on our website. Hereâ€™s how it works:

1. **Click on the "Store Locator" Button**: On our homepage or the navigation bar, you will find a **"Store Locator"** button.
2. **Enter Your Location**: Once you click the button, a prompt will ask you to enter your **zip code** or **city** to search for stores near you.
3. **View the Results**: After entering your location, the website will display a list of nearby stores, including their names, addresses, and contact information.
4. **Find the Nearest Store**: Use the map to see the exact location of the stores or view the detailed list for directions.

## Features of the Store Locator

- **Easy-to-Use Interface**: The store locator is user-friendly and simple to navigate. You only need to input your location, and it will automatically find the nearest stores that carry Lipton products.
- **Accurate Results**: The locator uses real-time data to ensure that the information about store locations is accurate and up-to-date.
- **Comprehensive Store Information**: Each store result includes detailed contact information, such as the address, phone number, and store hours.
- **Map Integration**: The store locator includes an interactive map feature, so you can view the store's exact location and get directions directly from the website.

## Access the Store Locator

To access the **Store Locator**, simply:

1. **Navigate to the Lipton Homepage**.
2. **Click on the "Store Locator" Button** in the navigation bar or footer.
3. **Follow the Instructions** to find stores near you.
